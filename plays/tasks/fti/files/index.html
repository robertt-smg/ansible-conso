Important Announcement

Dear business and sales partners,

After careful consideration, we have decided to exit the flight ticket consolidator business as of December 31, 2024.

after we withdrew from the flight ticket consolidator business on December 31, 2024, the operational business of FTI Ticketshop GmbH i.L. has now been completely discontinued and the company has entered a solvent liquidation proceeding in accordance with the provisions of the German Limited Liability Companies Act (GmbHG) since February 28, 2025. These are expressly not insolvency proceedings under the Insolvency Code.

In view of this, all of the company's IATA licenses were also terminated as of February 28, 2025 in accordance with the regulations of the International Air Transport Association (IATA).

All flight tickets that have already been issued and paid for naturally remain valid (subject to the fare conditions).

Furthermore, the external service center erf24 will be happy to help you contact the airlines directly on +49 89 710456040, Monday through Friday (except Bavarian holidays), from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

We would like to express our sincere thanks for your trust and the good cooperation in the past. It was our pleasure to assist you with flight bookings. We wish you all the best for the future!
