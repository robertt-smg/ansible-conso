Liebe Geschäfts- und Vertriebspartner*innen,

nachdem wir uns am 31. Dezember 2024 aus dem Flugticket-Consolidator Geschäft zurückgezogen hatten, wurde der operative Geschäftsbetrieb der FTI Ticketshop GmbH i.L. inzwischen vollständig eingestellt und die Gesellschaft befindet sich seit dem 28. Februar 2025 in einem solventen Liquidationsverfahren gemäß den Vorschriften des GmbH-Gesetzes (GmbHG). Vor diesem Hintergrund sind zum 28. Februar 2025 nach den Regularien der International Air Transport Association (IATA) auch sämtliche IATA Lizenzen der Gesellschaft beendet worden.

Alle bereits ausgestellten und bezahlten Flugticktes behalten selbstverständlich (im Rahmen der Tarifbedingungen) ihre Gültigkeit.

Wir bedanken uns herzlich für Ihr Vertrauen und die gute Zusammenarbeit in der Vergangenheit. Es war uns eine Freude, Ihnen bei Flugbuchungen zur Seite zu stehen. Wir wünschen Ihnen für die Zukunft alles Gute!

Important Announcement

Dear Sales Partners,

After careful consideration, we have decided to exit the flight ticket consolidator business as of December 31, 2024.

As of this date, we will unfortunately no longer be able to accept new orders, nor will we be able to process any subsequent administrative actions, such as changes or refunds. This means that all future changes and refunds after December 31, 2024, can only be requested directly by the passengers themselves, with your support as a travel agency or online booking portal, from the respective airlines. If you have been acting as an tour operators, you are already in direct contractual relationship with the airline and can make the corresponding changes.

We will make every effort to settle any refunds already submitted to the airlines, until the final termination of our IATA membership. After that, these claims can only be made directly with the respective airline, as described above.

All issued and paid flight tickets will remain valid (within the fare conditions) beyond December 31, 2024.

From January 2, 2025, the external service center erf24 will be available to assist you with direct contact to the airlines at the phone number +49 89 710456040, Monday to Friday (excluding Bavarian public holidays), from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Lufthansa will provide a special phone number for travel agencies, online booking portals, and tour operators for matters related to LUFTHANSA GROUP bookings issued on LUFTHANSA GROUP documents (LH, LX, OS, SN). This number can be obtained in the new year from our service provider erf24. Please note that this contact option is exclusively for travel agencies, online booking portals, and tour operators, and Lufthansa explicitly prohibits the passing of this number to end customers and passengers.

We sincerely thank you for your trust and the good cooperation in the past. It has been a pleasure to assist you with flight bookings. We wish you all the best for the future!
